Easily integrate Check Typo into your Chrome browser. A simple click on the word "Check," and you're on your way to flawless text

How to install

To install the Check Typo extension for Google Chrome, follow these simple steps:

  1. Click on the "Add to Chrome" button.
  2. A pop-up will appear asking if you want to add this extension to Chrome.
  3. Click "Yes" to confirm and install the extension.

Create an account

To create an account, follow these simple steps:

  1. Type in your email address and Password
  2. Click on Sign Up or Login if you already have an account
